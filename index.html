Process

No black-box launch.

The sprint is designed around source-of-truth control, QA, and human escalation. Automation goes live only after the risky cases are named.

Audit

Review support channels, repeated questions, macros, help-center gaps, and risk areas.

Map

Define automatable paths, restricted topics, owner approvals, and escalation rules.

Build

Configure workflows, answer sources, handoff triggers, QA prompts, and reporting.

Launch

Start with low-risk categories, monitor failures, and tune the system after real conversations.

Questions buyers ask

Clear boundaries before launch.

Will this replace support agents?

No. It is designed to reduce repetitive work and route sensitive or complex issues to humans.

Can you promise a fixed support-volume change?

No fixed percentage is promised. We measure deflection, response time, handoff accuracy, and unknown questions after launch.

What tools do you use?

We prefer the existing help desk and knowledge tools when possible, then add only what the workflow requires.

What is required from us?

A named owner, approved policies, access to support materials, and clear rules for restricted or sensitive topics.
