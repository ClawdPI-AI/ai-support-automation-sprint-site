Process

Five steps from intake to implementation decision.

The audit stays scoped to approved source material and produces a decision path before any customer-facing automation goes live.

Scope

Confirm support stack, channels, volume, owner, restricted topics, and approved materials.

Review

Inspect help content, macros, public support paths, repeated questions, and known handoff risks.

Map

Separate safe automation paths from human-owned or source-gap cases.

Test

Create QA prompts that challenge common, edge, angry, and restricted-topic questions.

Decide

Recommend no launch, limited pilot, or implementation sprint with access checklist.
