Likely paths

What the audit would map first

  • Return eligibility and return-start instructions.
  • Order edits before fulfillment: size/color swaps, address updates, cancellations, discount-code changes.
  • Missing or incorrect items.
  • Carrier-marked-delivered but not received.
  • Damaged products requiring photos.
  • Delivery timing and order-processing expectations.
  • Loyalty/app/account questions.
Safe opportunities

Where automation could help

  • Build pre-fulfillment order-edit triage: "swap size/color", "update shipping address", "cancel order", and "apply discount code" only when fulfillment status permits.
  • Create a return-eligibility assistant that checks public criteria before directing customers to returns.
  • Build damaged/missing item intake that asks for required order details and photos, then hands off to a human.
  • Create a carrier-delivered package workflow that explains the 2-business-day wait and carrier-claim requirement before escalation.
  • Add reporting for repeated "discount code", "sample final sale", "third-party retailer", and loyalty/app issues.
Human-owned

Risk areas to keep human

  • Refund exceptions and out-of-policy returns.
  • Missing package cases after carrier claim.
  • Product safety, supplement sensitivity, or health claims.
  • Account deletion/privacy requests.
  • High-frustration customers or public social complaints.
