Likely paths

What the audit would map first

  • Order status and WISMO questions.
  • Personalization, engraving, initials, birthstones, and custom product details.
  • Shipping timing for gifts and time-sensitive occasions.
  • Damaged, broken, or missing jewelry.
  • Sizing, fit, and material questions.
  • Product recommendations for gifting occasions.
Safe opportunities

Where automation could help

  • Create guidance documents for personalization questions that require exact source material.
  • Build safe self-service flows for order status, shipping windows, product care, and basic sizing.
  • Add escalation rules for damaged items, missed gift deadlines, emotionally sensitive gifts, and customization disputes.
  • Create QA tests for order-status accuracy, personalization edge cases, and empathetic tone.
  • Add weekly reporting for unknown questions, repeated personalization issues, and handoff failures.
Human-owned

Risk areas to keep human

  • Customization disputes or buyer remorse.
  • Lost, broken, or damaged sentimental gifts.
  • Angry customers around missed birthday, wedding, memorial, or holiday deadlines.
  • Refund or replacement decisions outside written policy.
  • Any unclear answer that could affect engraving, spelling, dates, or personalization.
